Your Nervous System — These questions reveal how your nervous system has learned to protect you. Answer honestly. Answer from your gut, not your head.

Question 1 of 7

When you feel overwhelmed, what is your first instinct?

Choose the one that feels most true right now. Go with your gut.

Question 2 of 7

When conflict arises with someone close to you, what do you most often do?

Think about your most recent experience of conflict. What actually happened, not what you wish you had done.

Question 3 of 7

When you finally have time to rest, what actually happens?

Choose the one that feels most true right now. Go with your gut.

Question 4 of 7

When you think about what YOU need, what actually happens inside?

Not what others need. What YOU need. Notice what comes up before you answer.

Question 5 of 7

Where does stress show up in your body — and what is usually triggering it?

Pay attention to the trigger, not just the sensation. Both matter.

Question 6 of 7

When you make a promise to yourself — a goal, a routine, a change — what usually happens?

Be honest. This is one of the most important questions in the assessment.

Question 7 of 7

Which of these feels most true about you right now?

Answer from the part of you that is most tired. The part that has been carrying the most.
